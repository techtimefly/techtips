---
title: Write prompts that actually work
category: ai-prompt
summary: A four-part formula that gets consistent results from any AI image tool.
effort: 5 min
order: 1
---

- Start with the subject first: who or what is in the scene, described concisely.
- Add style and medium next: "digital painting", "photorealistic", "watercolor illustration", "3D render".
- Include lighting and mood: "golden hour", "dramatic side lighting", "soft diffused light", "neon glow".
- Close with technical quality boosters: "highly detailed", "sharp focus", "8k resolution", "cinematic composition".
- Avoid negatives in your main prompt — use a negative prompt field if the tool offers one.
- Test one variable at a time: change only the style or only the lighting per iteration to learn what each word does.
