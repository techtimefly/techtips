---
title: Iterate your way to the perfect image
category: ai-prompt
summary: How to refine AI-generated images without starting from scratch every time.
effort: 3 min
order: 2
---

- Save your seed number when you get a result you like — it locks the random starting point so you can tweak from there.
- Make small edits to one word at a time rather than rewriting the whole prompt.
- Use image-to-image mode to hand the AI a rough sketch or reference photo as a starting point.
- Add specificity to weak areas: if the hands look wrong, describe exactly what the hands should be doing.
- Use weighting syntax if your tool supports it — e.g. "(purple:1.4)" emphasises that word more strongly.
- Keep a prompt log (a simple notes file) so you can revisit winning combinations across sessions.
